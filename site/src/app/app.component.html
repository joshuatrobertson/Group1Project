DistractAbility: Welcome
Welcome to DistractAbility!

DistractAbility highlights the negative impacts of COVID-19 on people’s ability to focus.

DistractAbility is a game with two rounds of a brain-training task. The first round is ‘normal’ however the second round is filled with distractions. Your task is to complete each round to the best of your ability.

Your score for each round will be displayed at the end of the second round. You have the option to press ‘see statistics’ if you wish to compare your scores with the average scores of other users.

Login with your email to be taken to the first round of the game.
